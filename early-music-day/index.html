Early Music Day — Novi Sad

Early Music Day in Novi Sad is a special project combining artistic residency, concerts, education, and research, created in collaboration with international artists.

The 2026 edition focuses on the work of guitarist and lutenist Rafael Arjona, who will spend time in Novi Sad working with local musicians, students, and audiences.

What happens during Early Music Day

  • Artistic residency and rehearsals
  • Workshops and masterclasses
  • Recording and documentation
  • Public concert
  • Educational outreach
